Playable canvas poem

Lecha Dodi: Shabbat Sparks

Walk the shoreline, gather twenty sparks, and let the whole scene deepen into Shabbat.

Come, greet the Sabbath bride.

Move with arrow keys or A/D. The first sparks ask reflective questions that you type answers for, and later sparks ask you to speak or sing into the microphone. Collect all twenty to bring in Shabbat. On the right side, use Up/Down or W/S to move into the ocean like a mikveh. Press Space or Enter to begin, P to pause, R to restart, and F for fullscreen.
